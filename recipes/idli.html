Idli

Description

Idli is a soft, fluffy, and savory steamed cake made from fermented rice and lentil batter, a staple breakfast food in South India, often served with chutney and sambar.

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • ½ teaspoon mustard seed
  • ½ teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1 teaspoon split Bengal gram (chana dal)
  • 10 fresh curry leaves

Steps

  1. Heat vegetable oil in a pan over medium-low heat.
  2. Add yogurt and carrot to the mixture and mix well.
  3. Grease an idli pan and add water to steam.
  4. Meanwhile, add fruit salt to the batter, mix well.
  5. Steam the idli for 10 minutes.
